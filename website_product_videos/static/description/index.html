Website Product Videos With Multi Images

Now describe your products with videos because videos always make more understanding.

The module brings the feature of videos on the product page to Odoo website. Now, you can add multiple product videos also to the product image carousel on product page to make it more informative and attractive for customers.

Information

https://webkul.com/blog/odoo-website-product-videos-with-multi-images

List of Features

Multiple videos with multi images

Provide feature to add/show multiple products videos using video links on website.


YouTube videos

It supports embed youtube video format.


Vimeo videos

It supports embed vimeo video format.


Use videos as product description

Provide feature to use multiple product's videos as product description.


Customize video

Admin can update video attributes as per need.


Play videos in a pop-up window

User can play the video in a pop-up window.

Videos as product Description

Enable "Use as product description" option to use video as product description.

Play videos in a pop-up window

Enable "Allow Popup Video" option to play video in a pop-up window.

Enable product multi videos feature on your website in one step.
